Study Tips

  • Create a plan: Set exam dates and daily goals, and start 2–3 months before the exam.
  • Use past exams: Practice with previous Regents tests to learn question style and time management.
  • Focus on weakness: Spend extra time on topics you miss most often.
  • Take practice tests: Simulate exam conditions with full-length, timed practice.
  • Avoid cramming: Short, regular study sessions work better than last-minute all-nighters.

Exam-Specific Advice

Regents: Show all work, use JMAP for math questions, and review Barron's practice exams.

AP exams: Study past FRQs from AP Central and follow the Course and Exam Description (CED).

SAT: Practice with digital SAT tools, use Khan Academy, and review vocabulary consistently.
